SureSource Launch

Overview

Launch is a tool built into the Insight portal that allows form builders to create clinical trial forms with a drag and drop interface.

Our form design team would take months building a clinical trial study with outdated form design tools, and we were exploring ways to make this build process quicker so we could handle more clinical trial studies.

We did extensive research with our form design team to find out what they liked about the old software and what they saw as pain points. Through this process, we were able to build software that was designed for their preferred workflow.

Form designers could now easily drag clinical controls onto the stage and quickly made edits.

Dragging a control onto the stage

Clicking edit on a control would bring up edit mode. Form designers could now edit pre-made controls quicker than ever.

Edit mode

The import study feature was a huge improvement to the old form designer software our team had used before. Form designers could now import studies that closely resembled a study they were working on and then make small edits. This increased the study build time so we could handle a larger quantity of studies.
