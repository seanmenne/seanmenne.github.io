SureSource Insight

Overview

SureSource Insight is a web-based portal that is used to manage and view all the collected data from a clinical trial site. It solves two major problems while conducting a clinical research trail:

In the past clinical trials were done on paper. Over time this led to incorrect data and once a study was finished it would take months to enter all that data into a database. Data discrepancies on paper forms would be shown by adding a colored post it note next to that data field, and sometimes those note would just fall off and never be retrieved.

With Insight monitors are now able to see trial data instantly from the site and can raise data discrepancies by opening the form and adding a query. Pharmaceutical companies can now get their data quicker and with less errors.

Monitors are hired by a pharmaceutical company and assigned clinical trial sites to look over during the full trial. Most monitors oversee multiple sites across different states which leads to a lot of traveling. Travelling monitors can add a lot of cost to the study and cause monitor burn out.

With data visualization monitors can easily keep track of all subjects, data discrepancies, and the overall site health without the need to travel on site. This helps cut costs, address issues more quickly and give them more time to focus on sites that need more attention
