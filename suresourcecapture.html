SureSource Capture

Overview

SureSource Capture allows clinical researchers to improve data quality and availability during a clinical trial process. Capture features powerful data validation checking that reduces data errors and cuts cost. Every interaction, including writing, is done with a stylus to keep the experience consistent with using actual paper.

Capture interface

Query, Validation and Change Management

Bring query, validation, and change management into Capture was the most requested feature at Clinical Ink. Before, monitors would have to leave their tablet and view queries inside the portal on a desktop computer. This feature now allows monitors to view and fix queries, validations, and changes directly on their tablet in a step by step flow. This dramatically increased the rate of queries, validations, and changes being addressed and closed, speeding up the entire clinical trial process.

Query, validation, and change management flow

CI Sync

Before CI Sync, if a user wanted to get the most recent data on a form they would have to tap the refresh icon before opening a form. We wanted to solve that problem with CI Sync. CI Sync is a system tray application that enables synchronization services to occur in the background and allow users to get the most recent version of each form without delay, which greatly improved the overall user experience. Also, When a user opened the Capture application they would have to wait while the studies assigned to them were downloading, but with CI Sync users can now enter any study while forms download in the background making the application feel much faster.

CI Sync

Check in / Check out form update

The old check in / check out form experience was a major pain point in Capture. When a user would check out a form a modal would pop up and the user would have to wait for it to finish downloading before they could check out another chart. When you're checking out multiple charts this could become very time consuming and disruptive. We decided to move the form downloading process out of the way and into a tray at the bottom of the application. Now when a user checks out a form the tray slides up indicating what forms are downloading and the user is still able to move around the application.

Old downloading modal that blocked the view of other charts

Updated tray design gives users the ability to move around the product while charts are downloading

Voice Input

Moving forward on our current mission to speed up the clinical trial process, and capture accurate data we are now adding voice as an option for data input. Voice input has continually been one of the most requested features, and through user research, we've found that an overwhelming amount of our users consider it essential. A user can now tap on the microphone icon next to the fields that are voice enabled and quickly input data.
