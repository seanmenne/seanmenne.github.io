Office Practicum cOPilot

Overview

The old Office Practicum patient portal was facing scalability and security concerns, and it was reliant on a dated and confusing CMS system. Patients frequently voiced their concern that they were having a hard time navigating the portal and since the portal was not responsive, they could not access important clinical information on their mobile devices.

cOPilot Patient Portal

Utilizing our patient engagement group, we discovered that our parents preferred to use websites on their mobile phone compared to a laptop by 75%. With this data, we wanted to rebuild our patient portal from the ground up with a responsive design in mind.

Patient medication list

Message attachments and threads were also a feature request that our old patient portal could not handle. Parents could now easily view messages all in one thread and add different types of attachments to their messages such as images, video, and audio.

Parents can send and receive messages and add attachments

We also updated the appointment view layout. It's now easier to view your past appointments and their outcomes. Confirming an appointment could also be done with just one tap or click.

Appointment list showing scheduled and past appointments

cOPilot Control Panel

The control panel was designed to give our physicians a high-level view of all their organizations and practices. From the control panel, physicians could manage their staff, link new practices, and deactivate practices that might have merged with others.

Control panel view showing organization cards with indiviual practices

Within the practice settings, we wanted to give our physicians full control over how their patient portal looked, and control what they wanted to show and hide, based on what their practice offered. From the practice settings, physicians could set up office hours, upload a custom logo, create custom notifications, view activity reports and enable and disable features.
